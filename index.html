About Me

John is on a mission to help institutions of education and government achieve more results with fewer (and better) resources. Trained as a graphic and web designer, he has a unique 8-year background that spans graphic design, user experience design, event planning, marketing, advertising, data management and web development.


He pioneered the graphic design and computer science program at the Arrow Center for Education (a high school for children with educational and emotional challenges) in 2015. Additionally, he has had a few speaking engagements in which he led UX and design workshops for non-profit organizations and web developer groups.


Currently, he is a Data Manager at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County.
